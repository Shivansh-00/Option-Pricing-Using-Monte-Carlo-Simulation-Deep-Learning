# Viva-Ready Answers

**Q: Why add DL on top of Monte Carlo?**  
DL learns residual errors and accelerates convergence with fewer simulated paths.

**Q: How do you ensure explainability?**  
We use SHAP for feature attribution and a RAG pipeline to ground answers in finance theory.

**Q: How do you handle regime shifts?**  
Regime classifiers inform model selection and risk flags in the dashboard.
